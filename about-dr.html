About Dr. Poornima

Dr. POORNIMA, MBBS, DGO, FRM

13+ Years Of Experience

QUALIFICATIONS

  • MBBS
  • DGO (Diploma in Obstetrics and Gynaecology)
  • FRM (Fellowship in Reproductive Medicine)
  • Fellowship in Ultrasonography
  • Fellowship in Cosmetic Gynecology

Dr. POORNIMA is the Chief Gynaecologist, Obstetrician, Infertility Specialist, and Laparoscopic Surgeon at POORNIMA FERTILITY CENTRE, Karimnagar.

  • She completed her MBBS from MNR Medical College in 2011, and her post-graduation (DGO) from KMC - Kakatiya Medical College in 2015.
  • Between 2015 and 2018, Dr. Poornima underwent advanced training in prenatal genetic evaluation, infertility, ultrasonography, and minimally invasive gynaecology & laparoscopy under the guidance of renowned doctors.
  • In 2018, she established her own clinic in Karimnagar, where she has successfully treated over 300 fertility couples and conducted more than 500 successful deliveries.
  • From 2022 to 2025, she served as a Senior Consultant at Ferty9 Karimnagar. During this tenure, she performed over 300 IUIs, 450 OPUs, and 550 FETs, and specialized in managing challenging cases such as thin endometrium and recurrent IVF failures.
  • Dr. Poornima further honed her skills by completing a Fertility Fellowship Program in Chennai under the esteemed Dr. Jayakannan at GFC Centre, Kodambakkam, Chennai.
  • She has also completed comprehensive Cosmetic Gynecology courses in both surgical and non-surgical fields, under the guidance of Dr. Ayman El Attar, MD, in association with the American Aesthetic Association.

She is also member of:

  • FOGSI (The Federation of Obstetric and Gynaecological Societies of India)
  • Life Member of ISAR (Indian Society of Assisted Reproduction) – She is actively involved in the ISAR community and has participated in many social awareness programs executed on behalf of ISAR.
  • Life Member of ICOG (Indian College of Obstetricians and Gynaecologists)

She regularly conducts Free Health Checkup Camps, Awareness Programs, and Infertility Camps at various hospitals, demonstrating her commitment to community health.

Throughout her Professional Journey, she has demonstrated Expertise in Obstetrics, Gynaecology, and Infertility.

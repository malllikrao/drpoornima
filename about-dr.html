About Dr. Poornima

Dr. POORNIMA, MS(OBG)

14+ Years Of Experience

QUALIFICATION

  • MBBS, MS - Obstetrics and Gynaecology, Certified Advanced Laparoscopy and Ultrasonography
  • Dr. POORNIMA is the Chief Gynaecologist, Obstetrician, Infertility Specialist and Laparoscopic Surgeon at POORNIMA FERTILITY CENTRE, Karimnagar.
  • She Completed her MBBS from Osmania Medical College in 2011. Completed MS(OBG) in Obstetrics and Gynaecology from Osmania University in 2018.
  • Underwent advanced training in prenatal genetic evaluation, infertility, ultrasonography, and minimally invasive gynaecology & laparoscopy under the guidance of renowned doctors.
  • From 2017-2021 she worked as Assistant to CHIEF CONSULTANT SURGEON in Government General Hospital, and Singlehandedly Incharge of Gynaecology Department.
  • In 2021-2022 she worked as Consultant Gynaecologist in SURESH Hospital.
  • In 2023 she worked as Consultant Gynaecologist in RAMDAS MOHAN & LAPAROSCOPIC Hospital.
  • In 2024 she had done observership in surgical Gynaecology Unit in YASHODA.
  • She underwent advanced training at JIPMER, Pondicherry.
  • Underwent Ultra-sonography Training at JIPMER, Pondicherry.
  • Underwent FOGSI Certified Advanced Laparoscopy Training Centre.
  • Fellowship in Infertility.
  • Mentors in reproductive medicine.
  • She had presented papers at VARIOUS CONFERENCES.
  • She has been actively involved in various medical camps and awareness programs.

She is also member of:

  • FOGSI
  • Life Member of ISAR (Indian Society of Assisted Reproduction)
  • Life Member of ICOG (Indian College of Obstetricians and Gynaecologists)

She conducted Free Health checkup camps, awareness and infertility camps at various hospitals.

Throughout her Professional Journey, she has demonstrated Expertise in Obstetrics, Gynaecology, and Infertility.

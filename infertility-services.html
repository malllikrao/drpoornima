Infertility Services

Ovulation Induction (OI)

Ovulation Induction (OI) uses fertility medications to stimulate egg release, helping women with irregular cycles or enhancing ovulation in those with regular cycles to increase the chances of pregnancy.

Pre-conception Counselling

A preconception consultation helps women assess risks and modify factors like folic acid intake, smoking cessation, and alcohol reduction to enhance maternal and fetal outcomes before pregnancy.

IUI (Intra Uterine Insemination)

Intrauterine Insemination (IUI) is an infertility treatment that involves placing specially prepared sperm directly into the uterus, enhancing the chances of sperm meeting an egg during ovulation to achieve pregnancy.

Psychological Counselling

Psychological counseling for Infertility focuses on emotional healing, normalizing experiences, improving relationships, managing stress, promoting acceptance, and facilitating grief, ultimately enhancing well-being and outcomes for couples struggling with Infertility.

Fertility Evaluation

Fertility evaluation assesses both partners' potential through physical exams, medical histories, and semen analysis for men. Women are evaluated for ovulation via blood tests and ultrasounds of the ovaries.

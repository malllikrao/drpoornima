Infertility Services

Pre-Marital Fertility Assessment & Counselling

This service offers comprehensive fertility health check-ups and personalized counseling for couples before marriage. It helps identify any potential fertility issues early on, addresses concerns about reproductive health, and educates partners on family planning, genetic risks, and healthy conception practices, ensuring they start their journey together well-informed and prepared.

Ovulation Induction (OI)

Ovulation Induction is a medical treatment designed for women who have irregular or absent ovulation. It involves using specific medications to stimulate the ovaries to produce and release one or more eggs. This process helps to regulate the menstrual cycle and synchronize egg release, significantly increasing the chances of conception, either naturally or in conjunction with timed intercourse or IUI.

Intrauterine Insemination (IUI)

IUI is a less invasive fertility treatment where specially prepared and concentrated sperm are directly placed into the woman's uterus around the time of ovulation. This procedure bypasses the cervix and places a higher concentration of healthy sperm closer to the egg, increasing the chances of fertilization. It is often recommended for mild male factor infertility, unexplained infertility, or cervical mucus issues.

Treatment For Unexplained Infertility

Unexplained infertility refers to cases where standard diagnostic tests do not reveal a clear cause for a couple's inability to conceive. Our approach involves a personalized, step-wise treatment plan that may include advanced diagnostic evaluations, ovulation induction, IUI, or progression to more advanced reproductive technologies like IVF. We aim to find the most effective path to pregnancy when the cause isn't immediately apparent.

Pre-conception Counselling

Pre-conception counseling provides essential guidance and advice for individuals and couples planning to conceive. This service focuses on optimizing health before pregnancy, addressing crucial aspects like nutrition, lifestyle adjustments, identification of potential genetic risks, and management of pre-existing medical conditions. The goal is to ensure that fastest and healthiest possible start for both the pregnancy and the baby.

Psychological Counselling

Navigating the emotional complexities of infertility and fertility treatments can be incredibly challenging. Our psychological counseling services offer dedicated emotional and mental health support for individuals and couples. We provide a safe, confidential space to discuss fears, anxieties, and stress, offering coping strategies, relaxation techniques, and guidance to enhance overall well-being throughout your fertility journey.

Fertility Evaluation

A comprehensive fertility evaluation is the foundational step in understanding and addressing infertility. This involves a thorough assessment of both male and female partners through a series of specialized tests, including hormone level checks, semen analysis, imaging studies (like ultrasound or HSG), and medical history review. This detailed evaluation helps to accurately diagnose the underlying causes, forming the basis for a targeted and effective treatment plan.

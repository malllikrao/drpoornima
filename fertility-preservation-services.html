Fertility Preservation Services

Preservation for Cancer Patients

Cancer treatments like chemotherapy and radiation can significantly damage reproductive organs and impair fertility. Our fertility preservation services offer a crucial opportunity for cancer patients to safeguard their reproductive future. We provide options such as egg freezing, sperm freezing, or embryo freezing before treatment begins, giving them the chance to have biological children once they have recovered and are ready to start a family.

Rheumatology And SLE (Lupus) Patients

For individuals managing autoimmune diseases such as Rheumatoid Arthritis or Systemic Lupus Erythematosus (SLE), fertility can be a concern. Certain medications used to manage these conditions, or the diseases themselves, can potentially affect reproductive health. Our fertility preservation services provide a vital option for these patients to proactively store their eggs or sperm, protecting their future reproductive options from potential damage or decline.

Industrial And Coal Mine Workers

Individuals working in industrial settings or coal mines are often exposed to environmental toxins, chemicals, heavy metals, or radiation that can negatively impact fertility. This specialized fertility preservation service allows workers in such hazardous environments to proactively preserve their reproductive material (eggs or sperm). By doing so, they can protect their ability to have children in the future, before potential occupational hazards cause irreparable damage to their fertility.

For Personal Reasons (Delayed Childbearing)

Many individuals and couples choose to delay parenthood due to career aspirations, personal goals, or simply not feeling ready to start a family. As age progresses, natural fertility declines. Our fertility preservation options, including egg freezing for women and sperm freezing for men, offer the flexibility to preserve younger, healthier gametes. This provides peace of mind and more options for future family planning when the time is right.

Genetic Conditions

For individuals or couples concerned about passing on specific genetic conditions or those whose own genetic conditions might impact their fertility, fertility preservation offers crucial solutions. This service can involve freezing eggs or sperm for future use with preimplantation genetic testing (PGT) to select unaffected embryos. It ensures that individuals can achieve a healthy pregnancy and minimize the risk of their offspring inheriting genetic diseases.
